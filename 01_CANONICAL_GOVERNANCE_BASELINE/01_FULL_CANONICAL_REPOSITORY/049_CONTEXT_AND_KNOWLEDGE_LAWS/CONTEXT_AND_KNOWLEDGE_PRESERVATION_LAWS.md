# CONTEXT AND KNOWLEDGE PRESERVATION LAWS™

Date: 20260610-220357
Status: Canonical

## Preserve More Than Outcomes
Preserve:
- What happened
- Why it happened
- What was considered
- What was rejected
- What remains open
- What risks exist
- What assumptions exist
- What changed
- What should happen next

## Knowledge Types
- Facts
- Decisions
- Rules
- Standards
- Directives
- Assumptions
- Tradeoffs
- Exceptions
- Rejected alternatives
- Lessons learned

## Rule
Future projects should not have to guess why something exists.
