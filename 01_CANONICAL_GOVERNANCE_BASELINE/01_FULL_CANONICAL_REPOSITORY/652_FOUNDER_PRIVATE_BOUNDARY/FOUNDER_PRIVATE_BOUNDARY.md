# FOUNDER PRIVATE BOUNDARY™

Date: 20260610-224911
Status: Hard Rule / Security Boundary

## Rule
Brian's founder/private OS, website, Vault, admin, governance, and internal activity must remain isolated from normal users.

## Users May Access
Only what their identity, role, entitlement, consent, and permission profile allows.

## Users Must Not Access
- Founder-only workflows
- Founder command/control areas
- Website administration
- Internal Vault records
- Other users' records
- Private business logic
- Private interaction history
- Approval/governance systems unless authorized
- Security controls unless authorized

## Validation Requirement
Every Lumen access path must prove:
- Who is requesting
- What channel they use
- What they can access
- What they cannot access
- Why permission was granted or denied
