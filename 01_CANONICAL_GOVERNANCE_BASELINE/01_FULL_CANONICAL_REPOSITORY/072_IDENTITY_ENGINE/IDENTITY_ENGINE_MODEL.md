Date: 20260610-222040
Status: Canonical Direction
Scope: Lumen OS, Website, Vault, ChatGPT/OpenAI Bridge, Future Native AI

## Purpose
Define identity as a universal Lumen continuity object that can operate across OS, website, Vault, and bridge access channels.

## Universal Rule
A user accessing Lumen through the website, OS, Vault, or ChatGPT bridge must resolve to a continuity-governed Lumen identity model.

## Preservation Rule
Identity records are additive and lineage-preserved.
Do not destructively overwrite identity context.
