# CONTEXT AND RATIONALE™

## Why This Repository Exists
ChatGPT projects can become too large.
When that happens, continuity must transfer cleanly.

## Why Containers Exist
To avoid one bloated transfer file.

## Why IDs Exist
To prevent orphaned records and filename-only tracking.

## Why Deltas Exist
To prevent copying giant histories repeatedly.

## Why Export Packets Exist
Future projects should upload only what is needed to resume.

## Hard Rule
A project transfer is not a summary.
It is a continuity mechanism.
