# TRANSFER SCHEMA™

## Every Transfer Contains
- TransferID
- ProjectID
- ParentProjectID
- OriginProjectID
- Timestamp
- Version
- ContinuationPoint
- ValidationStatus
- IntegrityStatus
- RequiredUploads
- RequiredContext
- OpenLoops
- Gaps
- Risks
- Blockers
- NextWork
- ArchiveReferences

## Rule
A transfer is not a summary.
A transfer is a continuity mechanism.
