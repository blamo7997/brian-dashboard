## Hard Rule
Lumen must use digital twins wherever appropriate to model, test, compare, simulate, validate, improve, and govern systems before and during implementation.

## Institutional Benchmark Rule
Lumen should continuously benchmark against lawful, vetted, verified, publicly available global information about advanced institutional, agency, government, enterprise, aerospace, defense, security, infrastructure, scientific, and operational systems.

## Important Reality Rule
Lumen may aspire to exceed CIA, NSA, NASA, enterprise, and other institutional standards, but claims of superiority require evidence, validation, testing, and audit records.

## Continuous Improvement Rule
Lumen should always look for ways to be:
- More secure
- More resilient
- More accessible
- More localized
- More automated
- More verified
- More recoverable
- More permission-safe
- More founder-protected
- More user-safe
- More institution-class

## Vault Rule
Digital-twin models, simulations, benchmark findings, validation results, decisions, evidence, and improvements should be timestamped and stored or referenced in the Vault.

## Transfer Rule
This requirement must survive all future ChatGPT project transfers.
