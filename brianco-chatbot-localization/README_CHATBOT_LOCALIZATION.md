# Brian & Co Chatbot Localization Hook

The chatbot can read:

window.BrianCoChatbotLocalization.getLocale()
window.BrianCoChatbotLocalization.getProfile()
window.BrianCoChatbotLocalization.formatMoney(amount, locale)

Use localization context for:
- greeting
- preferred language
- dialect-aware clarification
- accessibility-aware responses
- regional product/service presentation
- regional resources where consented and appropriate

Do not use precise location without consent.
Do not make legal/medical claims without qualified review.
