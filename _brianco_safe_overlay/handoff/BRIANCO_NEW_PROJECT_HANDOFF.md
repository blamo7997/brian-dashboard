## Chatbot Typo Intelligence Requirement

Brian & Co chatbot must automatically understand, normalize, and safely handle typos, misspellings, slang, shorthand, unclear wording, and accessibility-related input issues for all users in real time wherever feasible.

Applies to:

- Website chatbot
- Admin UI
- Customer portal
- Supplier portal
- Artisan portal
- Creator portal
- Influencer portal
- Investor portal
- Banker portal
- Lawyer portal
- SMS/MMS
- Email
- Future apps/platforms

Rules:

- Understand typos silently.
- Do not shame, mock, or overcorrect users.
- Preserve the user's likely meaning and emotional intent.
- Clarify only when the corrected meaning is risky, ambiguous, legal, financial, medical, account-changing, destructive, or could touch existing connections.
- Route corrected intent through fraud/scam, legal, approval, localization, accessibility, role, and founder green-check gates.
- Never use typo correction to bypass safety or approval checks.
